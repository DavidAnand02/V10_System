Instructions

1. Status Page: Track the rewards you set and earn from quests here. These include:

  • Stat Points: These contribute to your overall player level.
  • Player Effects: These provide boosts and special protections, either passive or active. Effects can be innate, granted by equipping a class/job or title. Titles are also achieved through quests.

2. Job Page: List the jobs you're interested in acquiring and leveling up. Acquiring a job means you can have job-specific skills and quests.

3. Skills Page: Track the level and progress of all your attained skills. You can toggle between mental and physical skills and track progress by hours or repetitions. To reach the max level of 100, you must input 10,000 hours or repetitions.

4. Quests Page: Add and manage your quests here. You can set a recurrence interval for quests, measured in seconds. When a quest's recurrence interval is completed, it will return to the active list. If you fail a quest, you will need to manually move it back to the active list when ready to try again. Rewards, such as Titles or Stat Points, will be reflected in the Status Page.
